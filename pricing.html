FAQ (Frenquently asked questions)

What are the differences between free and paid services?+

Free services include access to communal areas, Wi-Fi, and basic utilities. Paid services offer additional benefits such as private office spaces, dedicated desks, advanced printing services, and access to premium amenities.

How do I book a workspace?+

You can book a workspace through our website or mobile app by selecting your desired space and time. Paid members can also reserve private offices and meeting rooms in advance.

What are your hours of operation?+

Our workspaces are open 24/7 for paid members. Free members have access during standard business hours, which are from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.

Can I upgrade from a free membership to a paid one?+

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time through your account on our website or app. Simply select the membership plan that suits your needs and follow the instructions.

What amenities are included with paid memberships?+

Paid memberships include access to private offices, meeting rooms, advanced printing services, high-speed internet, premium coffee and snacks, and exclusive networking events.

What safety measures are in place at your workspaces?+

We prioritize the health and safety of our members. Our workspaces are regularly cleaned and sanitized, and we have implemented social distancing measures and contactless entry systems.

Do you offer any discounts for long-term memberships?+

Yes, we offer discounts for long-term memberships. Please contact our support team for more details on pricing and discounts for quarterly or annual plans.
